Reflection and Experiences

The most challenging part of the project was communication with my groupmates, other than that it was figuring out how to do the website exactly according to the specifications of my client. I enjoyed using Github, and I also enjoyed having a little bit of creative freedom in designing the website, but also having an exact plan. I'm definitely more confident in my CSS now, so I'm sure I'd be a lot faster. I do feel more confident working on real projects, even though I only worked on one website, I feel like my time working on that website was really good for making me think about what making a real website would be like.
